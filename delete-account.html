Delete Account — Virtue

Developer: Virtue Technologies

How to request deletion

  1. Open the Virtue app → Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  2. Or send an email to support@virtueapp.in with subject "Account Deletion Request". Include the email used to sign up.
  3. We will verify your identity and then schedule deletion.

What we delete

  • Account information (email, profile data)
  • Screen-time logs, block settings, commitments
  • Rewards and transaction history (user-visible)
  • Device-specific usage data tied to your account

What we may retain

Retained data (anonymized or limited) for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or bookkeeping: transaction records up to 90 days. Identifiers used for audit may be kept as required by law.

Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7–14 days. You’ll get confirmation by email when deletion completes.

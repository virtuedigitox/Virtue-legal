How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion in either of these ways:

  1. Open the Virtue app → Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  2. OR email us at virtue.digitox@gmail.com with the subject:
    "Account Deletion Request" and include the email or phone used for login.
Send Deletion Request Email

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your identity is verified, we permanently delete the following:

  • Your account information (email, profile details)
  • Your screen-time data and app usage logs
  • Your app block configurations and commitments
  • Your reward history and in-app transaction history
  • Your device-specific tracking & analytics tied to your account

What Data May Be Retained (Temporarily)

To comply with Indian law, prevent fraud, and maintain financial audit records, certain non-personal or legally required records may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion is completed. All retained data is either anonymized or restricted to mandatory compliance logs.

Processing Timeline

Your request will be processed within 7–14 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and associated data are deleted.
